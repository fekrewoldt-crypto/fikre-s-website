How to Use

  • Click a body region to add pain (starts at intensity 5)
  • Click and hold to increase intensity (up to 10)
  • Shift + Click to decrease intensity
  • Drag to rotate the 3D model (or use 360° mode)
  • Scroll to zoom in/out
  • Toggle M/F for male/female body shapes
  • Front/Back/360° buttons for different views
